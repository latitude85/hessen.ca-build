About Hessen

We employ a wide variety of market-neutral strategies allowing us to achieve superior risk-adjusted returns. Hessen places a strong emphasis on technology enhancing our traders’ ability to follow a large number of securities and identify short-term market inefficiencies.

In-house technology

Our trading platform was developed internally with trader input, allowing us to quickly adapt to new trading strategies as market conditions evolve. It also allows us to diligently manage risk throughout our portfolio.
