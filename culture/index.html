Culture

Hessen was founded by friends with the goal of creating a workspace built on mutual respect, engagement and fun.

Get to know us

Hessen is an independent trading firm based in Montreal. Since its founding in 2017, our team has steadily grown to include qualified and dedicated individuals from diverse professional backgrounds. Our discerning approach to new ideas and the integration of advanced trading technologies allows us to capitalize on expansive opportunities in North American equity markets with limited downside risk and volatility.

Meet our partners

Scott, Mickael, and Adam were first introduced in 2014 as colleagues before quickly becoming friends. Their complementary skills and shared values form the synergistic partnership behind Hessen. As founding partners, they are actively involved in the ongoing development of the firm’s trading technologies and growth. Their leadership philosophy encourages teamwork, openness and creativity.

Social

Happy hours, board game nights, outings: We believe in the importance of developing a sense of togetherness.

Stress-free space

Our office space was designed with that in mind. Expect to find plants, a foosball table and a fully-stocked bar to unwind and recharge.

Creativity

Our ability to innovate and adapt wouldn’t be possible without the free-flowing of ideas. Every individual has something unique to offer.

Meet our team

Hessen was founded by a group of friends and colleagues who epitomize a culture of teamwork that extends beyond the walls of our office. Outside of work, we often get together to explore and develop our shared-interests, such as wine tasting and friendly (though sometimes competitive) foosball games.
